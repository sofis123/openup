Team Open Up

Made up of a diverse group of people passionate about community building, the Open Up team designed an open and fun virtual community that aims to be an interactive and safe space wherein Middelland residents can explore their neighborhood and form new connections through shared interests, skills, and values.

The project is a partnership between the Digital Society School, De Middellander - Het Wijkbedrijf, and Stichting CAATS.
