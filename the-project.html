About Us

We are a team of four members who are Designers and Researchers. We are passionate about creating viable solutions towards a future that aligns with the UN sustainable development goals. Thus, the centre of our research and design have been tailored to address
Goal 9: Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure
Goal 10: Reduced Inequalities
Goal 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities
Goal 17: Partnership for the Goals
Together with Delfshaven Digital, CAAT, as trainees at DSS Hogeschool van Amsterdam, we worked on a project called "______”. With the hopes of creating a resilient and inclusive neighborhood, "______” connects residents together through a digital platform where they can discover their strengths, interest, and share their stories.
How can we create interaction among different residents in a fun and engaging way? We can unravel our talents and find opportunities in our own neighborhoods.
