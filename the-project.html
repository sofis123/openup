Context

IncluCity is a partnership between the Digital Society School, Delfshaven Digital, De Middellander Het Wijkbedrijf, and Stichting CAATS. The project is to be developed and tested in Middelland a neighborhood located in Rotterdam, and then be applied to other neighborhoods in the Netherlands and other international locations.

Maker Sprint

We are a team of four members who are Designers and Researchers. We are passionate about creating viable solutions towards a future that aligns with the UN sustainable development goals. Thus, the centre of our research and design have been tailored to address Goal 9: Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure Goal 10: Reduced Inequalities Goal 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities Goal 17: Partnership for the Goals Together with Delfshaven Digital, CAAT, as trainees at DSS Hogeschool van Amsterdam, we worked on a project called "______”. With the hopes of creating a resilient and inclusive neighborhood, "______” connects residents together through a digital platform where they can discover their strengths, interest, and share their stories. How can we create interaction among different residents in a fun and engaging way? We can unravel our talents and find opportunities in our own neighborhoods. If you any further questions, please don’t hesitate to contact us.
