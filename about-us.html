About Us

The Open Up team consists of designers and researchers from different disciplines and backgrounds. What binds them is their interest in digital transformation and dedication to supporting and building communities. During the 20 week traineeship, they merged those affinities together and built a digital tool that aspires to support the Middelland neighborhood, within the Delfshaven borough in Rotterdam.
